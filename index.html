JUMPING RABBIT

In Ontario, there's a restless rabbit jumping across scenes and styles. That's us: Jumping Rabbit, a label that distributes the best of the underground, no matter the tag. Here you'll find rock, pop, reggae, alternative, punk, kraut, noise, metal, experimental, etc. We love real music, the kind born in basements, garages, and small stages.
